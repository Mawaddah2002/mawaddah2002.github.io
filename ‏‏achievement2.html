My Participation in the UQU 2024 Enjaz Exhibition

I am very pleased to participate in the UQU 2024 Enjaz Exhibition for graduation projects, which was held at King Saud Hall at Umm Al-Qura University in Mecca on June 5, 2024. I had the opportunity to showcase my graduation project, "Tanafas," which represents the result of teamwork and dedication to promoting mental health awareness among youth.

Participation Details

  • Project Presentation: I presented "Tanafas" as an interactive educational tool aimed at helping young people understand social anxiety and how to cope with it. Through puzzles and interactive stories, players can explore real-life experiences and enhance their knowledge.
  • Interaction with Visitors: I was excited to engage with visitors, explaining the project's objectives and how it can contribute to improving mental health. The feedback was overwhelmingly positive, which motivated me to continue developing the game.
  • Fostering Collaboration: Through my participation in the exhibition, I was able to connect with peers and faculty, exchanging ideas and experiences. This type of collaboration enhances the spirit of innovation and contributes to the development of future projects.
  • Future Vision: My participation in the exhibition was an opportunity to see how my project can contribute to achieving Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 by developing educational games focused on mental health.

Conclusion

I consider my participation in the UQU Enjaz Exhibition for graduation projects a unique opportunity to showcase my passion for mental health and technology. I look forward to more opportunities to develop "Tanafas" and increase its positive impact on youth in the future.
